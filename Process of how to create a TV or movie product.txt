Process of how to create a TV or movie product.

#1 Create a subclass (AdminTVDetailss.java / AdminMovicDetails.java) which extends AdminProductDetails.java
#2 Customize subclass's own attribute (each subclass has its own attribute besides the public attributes)
#3 Customize subclass's constructor
#4 Override toProduct() method in subclass
#5 Implement method in AdminProductList (Line 59 & 62) to display Movie or TV dialog.
